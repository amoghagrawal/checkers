How to Play Checkers

1. Red moves first. You are playing as red pieces.

2. Click on your piece to select it, then click on a highlighted square to move.

3. Regular pieces can only move forward diagonally.

4. Jump over opponent's pieces to capture them.

5. When a piece reaches the opposite end of the board, it becomes a King.

6. Kings can move and capture both forward and backward.

7. Win by capturing all opponent's pieces!
